Overthrow Dota 2

About Overthrow

Overthrow is a custom game mode for Dota 2 that offers fast-paced, team-based action. Unlike traditional Dota 2 matches, Overthrow takes place in a smaller map, promoting frequent player encounters and combat.

Notable Features

  • Golden Experience: Coins appear throughout the game, providing bonus gold and experience when collected.
  • Item Delivery: Instead of couriers, items are dropped to players via parachute.
  • Center Combat: Players or teams controlling the map's center receive bonuses, making it a focal point of conflict.
