Nasi Goreng ya kan

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of cold, pre-cooked jasmine rice (best if left overnight in the fridge)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sambal oelek or chili paste (adjust to your preference)
  • 100 grams of chicken breast or shrimp (or both), cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 small carrot, julienned or diced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil or canola oil
  • Optional toppings: fried shallots, prawn crackers, cucumber slices, and a side of pickled vegetables (acar).

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Rice: If you're using freshly cooked rice, spread it out on a tray and let it cool down. Refrigerate for several hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Fry the Protein: In a wok or large skillet, heat oil and cook the chicken or shrimp. Remove and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry Garlic and Shallots: Add more oil to the wok and saut√© the garlic and shallot until fragrant.
  4. Add the Rice: Break up any clumps and stir-fry with the garlic and shallot.
  5. Add the Sauces: Pour in the kecap manis, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sambal oelek. Stir well.
  6. Push Rice to the Side: Make a well and crack in the eggs. Scramble and mix into the rice.
  7. Final Add-ins: Return the protein to the wok. Add carrots and green onions. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Seasoning: Add white pepper, adjust as needed.
  9. Serve: Plate the nasi goreng and garnish with optional toppings.
Enjoy your homemade Indonesian Nasi Goreng!
